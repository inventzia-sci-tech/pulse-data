# Contributing to pulse-data

Thank you for considering a contribution. To keep the dual-licensing model of
this project workable, every contribution must satisfy the conditions below.

## 1. DCO sign-off

Every commit you submit must be signed off, certifying the
[Developer Certificate of Origin (DCO) v1.1](https://developercertificate.org/).

Sign off automatically by committing with `-s`:

```
git commit -s -m "your message"
```

This appends a `Signed-off-by: Your Name <you@example.com>` line. CI rejects
pull requests that contain commits without sign-off.

## 2. Dual-licensing grant

By signing off on your commit you also agree that your contribution is
licensed under **both**:

- the GNU Affero General Public License v3.0 or later, **and**
- the Inventzia Commercial License (see `LICENSE-COMMERCIAL.txt`),

and that Inventzia Science and Technology Ltd. may distribute your
contribution under either license.

## 3. Original work

You confirm that the contribution is your original work, or that you have the
rights to submit it under the terms above. If your employer holds rights to
your work, please obtain written permission before contributing.

## Questions

For larger contributions or to discuss licensing in detail, please contact
**operations@inventzia.com** before opening the pull request.
